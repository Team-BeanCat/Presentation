The Problem:

The loss of species is a disaster for mankind.

This is because a lot of different species rely on each other for food, and when one is removed it can cause a cascading affect and other species can become extinct. This problem is caused by the widespread lack of thought towards the environment, these issues can include things like deforestation, global warming and pollution.
